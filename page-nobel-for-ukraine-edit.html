Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nobel Internship free?
YES, IT IS! Our goal is to provide equal education and growth opportunities to everyone, therefore, the Nobel Internship Program is completely free of charge.
Who is our perfect intern?
  • A 16+ year old interested in developing their skills for the 21st century global job market
  • Someone who wants to improve their conversational English
  • Someone who can commit 4h+ of scheduled activities per week
  • Someone who wants to meet people from different parts of the world
5 reasons to join internship?
In Nobel you will:
  1. learn and improve the essential hard and soft skills you need for your future;
  2. gain the experience of working in an international company;
  3. receive a certificate upon completion of the Internship;
  4. find the most awesome, smart, motivated, and interesting people from all over the world;
  5. boost your English level.
What do I need to have to participate in the Internship?
  • PC / Laptop (NO tablets / phones) - Windows / Apple / Chromebook
  • Headset with boom mic / Earphones with mic / Gaming headset
  • Working webcam
  • Noise-free environment
  • Stable internet connection
How do I join the Program?
  • Fill out the form;
  • Check your inbox;
  • Attend Group Interview & Orientation meeting;
  • Welcome onboard!

Nobel Reach: Uniting Ukraine with
Donated Laptops a Brighter Future!

Calling all Ukrainians in need of laptops and organizations looking to make a difference! Through Nobel Reach, we are proud to say that we have already facilitated the donation of 100+ laptops, and this number continues to grow each day. In the wake of the war, many Ukrainians face immense challenges, with safety concerns and exorbitant laptop prices hindering their ability to work, study, and communicate. Our mission is to bridge this gap by connecting individuals and organizations willing to donate laptops worldwide. Join us in empowering Ukraine's future, one laptop at a time. Together, we can make a lasting impact and create a brighter tomorrow for those in need.
